Thanks for visiting! We’d love for you to take a browse through our website and get in touch if there is anything we can help you with. We are experts in qualitative, quantitative and social media research - and if you are looking for something especially statistical, please visit at our sister company’s site: The Testing Panel

As a boutique agency, we partner with you to deeply understand your brands and consumers. We marry consumer truth and culture with your need to find the best solutions for your brands. At Vibrand, we believe in research that is actionable and anchored in the real world. That’s why we ensure hands-on director-level involvement on every single project, providing guidance, insight and a strategic lens throughout – right through to workshopping the outcomes with you.

When you choose Vibrand you choose a flexible, knowledgeable, highly experienced team who pride themselves on professionalism and delivering the best client service in the industry. We bring enthusiasm, flexibility and a ‘can do’ attitude to your business challenges.

Between us, we have worked across every category imaginable - if you know exactly what you want we will provide it and more, and if you don’t, we will work with you to help construct the best research solution available within your budget.

We provide all the ‘usual’ qual methods – and we do them very, very well. From brief to proposal, RQ to DG to choosing the right moderator, quick turn-around topline results to final presentations personally delivered by MD Stuart who draws on over 20 years’ experience to deliver extraordinary insights. We provide qual work throughout SA, and in over 20 other African countries. Through our partner network, we are also able to run multi-country Africa projects simultaneously – up to five countries at a time.

Our highly customised approach to quant work is specifically tailored to your project. We bring the methods we have learned from sensory work into the mix, and inevitably offer all that you’d expect with some surprising extras - conjoint, CATA and JAR analysis tend to come at no extra charge.

We will always go the extra mile for you, and that includes being highly competitive on cost.

We’d love to chat – drop us a line on info@vibrand.co.za
